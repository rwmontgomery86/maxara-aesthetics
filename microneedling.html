The Science

Collagen
Induction

Microneedling creates thousands of controlled micro-channels in the skin, triggering your body's natural wound-healing cascade. Growth factors flood the treatment area, signaling fibroblasts to produce fresh collagen and elastin fibers.

The result is visibly firmer, smoother, and more radiant skin that continues improving for months after treatment.

  • Micro-Channels — Trigger natural wound healing response
  • Collagen Synthesis — New structural fibers rebuild skin
  • Elastin Production — Restores bounce & firmness
  • Growth Factors — Accelerate cellular renewal

Targeted Results

What It
Treats

Microneedling addresses a wide range of skin concerns by working beneath the surface to rebuild and renew from within.

Fine Lines & Wrinkles

Stimulates collagen production to plump and smooth fine lines, restoring a firmer, more youthful skin surface over time.

Acne Scarring

Breaks down scar tissue and promotes new collagen formation, visibly reducing the depth and appearance of acne scars.

Enlarged Pores

Tightens and refines pore appearance through increased collagen around pore walls, creating a smoother, more polished complexion.

Uneven Texture

Resurfaces rough, uneven skin by accelerating cell turnover and encouraging the growth of fresh, smooth skin layers.

Hyperpigmentation

Encourages turnover of pigmented cells and promotes even melanin distribution for a clearer, more uniform skin tone.

Loss of Firmness

Rebuilds the skin's structural foundation by stimulating elastin and collagen production, restoring a lifted, supple feel.

Your Recovery

The Healing
Journey

Know what to expect as your skin heals and rebuilds. Follow your transformation day by day.

Treatment Day

Redness Like Mild Sunburn

Immediately after treatment, your skin will appear pink to red and feel warm and tight. This is a completely normal response as your skin begins its healing process. Most clients compare the sensation to a mild sunburn.

What To Do

  • Apply provided soothing serum
  • Avoid touching your face
  • No makeup for 24 hours
  • Stay out of direct sun

The Initial Phase

Peak Redness Subsiding

Redness and mild swelling are resolving. Your skin may feel tight, dry, and more sensitive than usual. This is your body's healing response in full effect as new collagen production begins beneath the surface.

What To Do

  • Gentle cleansing only with mild cleanser
  • No active ingredients (retinol, acids, vitamin C)
  • Keep skin hydrated with gentle moisturizer
  • Avoid heat, sweat & strenuous exercise

Renewal Begins

Light Flaking & Peeling

You may notice light flaking or dryness as old skin cells shed and new skin forms beneath. This is a sign of active renewal. The flaking is typically mild and can be managed with consistent moisturizing.

What To Do

  • Do NOT pick or pull at flaking skin
  • Continue gentle cleanser & moisturizer
  • Apply SPF 30+ daily
  • Avoid exfoliants & harsh products

Active Healing

Skin Softening & Brightening

Any flaking resolves and your skin starts to look noticeably smoother and brighter. The fresh skin beneath is being nourished by new collagen fibers forming deep in the dermis. You'll begin to see early results emerging.

What To Do

  • Continue your gentle skincare routine
  • SPF is mandatory when outdoors
  • Stay hydrated — drink plenty of water
  • Can gradually return to light activity

The Results

Full Results Emerging

Your skin is visibly firmer, smoother, and more radiant. Collagen continues building for weeks to come, with results improving progressively over the next 3-6 months as new fibers mature and strengthen.

Going Forward

  • Maintain daily SPF to protect your results
  • Gradually reintroduce your normal skincare
  • Schedule next session in 4-6 weeks for optimal results
  • Enjoy your renewed skin

Post-Treatment Care

Aftercare
Essentials

Proper aftercare maximizes your results and protects your healing skin. Follow these guidelines to get the most out of every microneedling session.

  • SPF 30+ sunscreen daily — non-negotiable
  • Gentle, sulfate-free cleanser only for 7 days
  • Avoid makeup for 24-48 hours post-treatment
  • No retinol, acids, or exfoliants for 5-7 days
  • Avoid direct sun, saunas & hot showers
  • Do not pick at any flaking skin

FAQ

Know Before
You Needle

Everything you need to feel confident before your first microneedling session.

Microneedling, also known as collagen induction therapy, uses fine, sterile needles to create controlled micro-injuries in the skin. These tiny channels trigger your body's natural healing response, stimulating the production of new collagen and elastin fibers for smoother, firmer, and more youthful-looking skin.

A professional-grade numbing cream is applied before every treatment, so most clients experience only mild pressure or a light prickling sensation. Comfort levels vary by individual and treatment area, but the numbing ensures the experience is very manageable.

Most clients see visible improvement after a single session, but for deeper concerns like acne scarring or significant texture issues, a series of 3-6 treatments spaced 4-6 weeks apart delivers the best results. We'll create a personalized plan based on your goals during your consultation.

Expect 1-3 days of redness similar to a mild sunburn. Most clients return to their daily routine within 24-48 hours. Light flaking may occur around days 4-5 as new skin emerges. Full healing typically completes within 7-10 days.

Yes! We offer microneedling paired with a HydraFacial for enhanced hydration and prep, or with our Jessner Peel for maximum collagen stimulation and resurfacing. These combination treatments deliver amplified results in a single session.

Microneedling is generally safe for all skin types and tones. Unlike some laser treatments, it doesn't rely on heat or light, making it a great option for darker skin tones. During your consultation, we'll evaluate your skin to ensure microneedling is the right fit for you.

Before: Avoid retinoids and exfoliants for 5-7 days, skip blood-thinning medications, and protect skin from sunburn. After: Use gentle cleanser and moisturizer only, apply SPF 30+ daily, avoid makeup for 24-48 hours, and skip active ingredients like retinol and acids for 5-7 days.

You'll notice improved texture and a fresh glow within 2-3 weeks as new collagen forms. Results continue improving for 3-6 months as collagen fibers mature and strengthen. Expect smoother texture, reduced scarring, smaller-looking pores, and firmer, more radiant skin overall.
