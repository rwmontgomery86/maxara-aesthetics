FAQ

Know Before
You Wax

Everything you need to feel confident before your first session.

We use premium hard wax for sensitive and delicate areas like the face and intimate zones, and high-quality soft wax for larger body areas like legs and back. Both are formulated for sensitive skin, minimizing irritation while ensuring thorough hair removal.

Hair should be at least a quarter-inch long — roughly the length of a grain of rice. This is typically about 2–3 weeks of growth after shaving. If your hair is too short, the wax may not be able to grip it effectively.

There is some discomfort, especially during your first session, but it is brief and very manageable. Most clients find that it gets easier with each visit as hair grows back finer and thinner. We use techniques and premium waxes specifically designed to minimize discomfort.

Most clients enjoy smooth skin for 3–6 weeks, depending on individual hair growth cycles. With regular waxing, hair typically grows back finer and sparser, extending the time between appointments. We recommend booking every 4–6 weeks for best results.

Absolutely. Our waxes are specifically formulated for sensitive skin. We also apply pre-wax oil to create a protective barrier and use post-wax serum to calm the skin immediately after treatment. If you have specific skin concerns, let us know and we will tailor the treatment accordingly.

Gently exfoliate the area 24 hours before your appointment to remove dead skin cells. Avoid sun exposure, tanning, and retinol products for at least 48 hours before your session. On the day of your appointment, skip lotions and oils on the area being waxed. Wear loose, comfortable clothing for body waxing.
