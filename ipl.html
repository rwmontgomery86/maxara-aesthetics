The Treatment

How IPL
Works on Skin.

Intense pulsed light uses broad-spectrum wavelengths to break up pigment and reduce visible redness, without damaging the skin's surface. You'll see clearer, more even tone over the days that follow.

  1. 01

    Consultation & Mapping

    We assess your skin tone, history, and goals, then map your treatment areas.

  2. 02

    Light, Layered Gently

    Calibrated pulses pass over each area in measured passes. You'll feel warmth, not pain.

  3. 03

    Renewal Over Days

    Pigment darkens and lifts away over 7–10 days while the skin heals.

Recommended Plan

Before & After Your Visit

Your Prep
and Aftercare.

A few steps in the weeks before your visit, and the days after.

01 · Before

PreparingYour Skin.

In the four weeks leading up to your visit.

  • Avoid sun & self-tanner.For four weeks before your visit. An active tan or burn delays your appointment.

  • Pause active ingredients.Retinoids, AHAs/BHAs, and benzoyl peroxide for 5–7 days before.

  • Arrive bare-faced.Clean skin only. No makeup, sunscreen, or moisturizer on treatment areas.

  • Disclose recent medications.Tell us about antibiotics, isotretinoin in the last 6 months, and any photosensitizing prescriptions.

02 · After

AfterYour Session.

In the 7–10 days following each session.

  • Wear sunscreen daily.Broad-spectrum SPF 30+, every day. Skipping it will undo the treatment.

  • Gentle cleansers only.Skip exfoliants, retinoids, and active ingredients for five days.

  • Cool down 24 hours.No hot water, saunas, steam rooms, or strenuous workouts the day of.

  • Let pigment shed naturally.Spots may darken before they lift. Don't pick or scrub. The process takes 7–10 days.
