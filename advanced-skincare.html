Beyond the Surface

What is Advanced
Skincare?

Our advanced skincare menu brings together non-invasive technologies and precision techniques that target concerns traditional facials can't reach — from skin laxity and scarring to stubborn tags and textural irregularities.

Each treatment is selected for its safety profile, effectiveness, and ability to deliver visible results with minimal downtime. Your provider will guide you to the right option based on your unique goals.

Targeted Results

What We
Treat

Our advanced treatments address a range of specific skin concerns, delivering visible improvement where it matters most.

Skin Laxity

Sagging or looseness in the face, neck, jawline, and body that benefits from non-invasive tightening and lifting.

Fine Lines & Wrinkles

Visible aging signs around the eyes, mouth, and forehead that respond to collagen stimulation and skin renewal.

Scarring

Acne scars, surgical scars, and textural irregularities that can be softened through controlled plasma energy treatments.

Skin Tags

Small benign growths that are cosmetically bothersome, removed quickly and precisely with plasma pen technology.

Uneven Texture

Rough, bumpy, or pockmarked skin surface that can be smoothed and refined through targeted plasma treatments.

Enlarged Pores

Visible pores and dull, tired-looking skin that benefits from collagen-boosting treatments for a tighter, brighter appearance.

Non-invasive skin tightening using focused ultrasound. Targets deeper tissue layers to lift and firm the brow, jawline, chin, neck, and chest — without needles or downtime. Most clients notice gradual improvement over 8–12 weeks as collagen rebuilds.

  • Focused ultrasound technology
  • Targets brow, jawline, chin, neck & chest
  • No needles or downtime

Skin Tag Removal

$50+  ·  15+ Minutes

Quick, precise removal using the Plamere™ Plasma Pen. Starting at $50 for one tag and $10 per additional tag. A brief assessment is followed by targeted treatment. A tiny dry scab forms and sheds naturally within 7–10 days. Return to normal activities right away.

  • Plamere™ Plasma Pen technology
  • Brief assessment before treatment
  • Normal activities immediately after
  • Scab sheds naturally in 7–10 days
  • For benign-appearing skin tags only
  • Suspicious tags declined & referred out

Scar Revision

$200+  ·  45 Minutes

  • Plamere™ Plasma Pen technology
  • Supports natural skin renewal
  • Softens texture over multiple sessions
  • Small crusts resolve naturally

Plasma Pen
