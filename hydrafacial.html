The Process

How HydraFacial
Works

You'll leave with a fresh, hydrated glow — smooth, clear, and ready for anything, with no downtime.

Most guests see a visible difference after one visit.

1

Cleanse

A gentle, exfoliating cleanse sweeps away dead cells and buildup to reveal fresh, touch-smooth skin. It perfectly preps your complexion for deeper treatment without irritation.

2

Extract

Precise vortex suction lifts oil, congestion, and impurities from pores — no pinching, no redness. Skin looks clearer and feels lighter immediately.

3

Hydrate

Targeted serums — think antioxidants, peptides, and deep hydrators — are infused to replenish and balance. You'll leave with a dewy, lasting glow.
