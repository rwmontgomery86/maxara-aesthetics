FAQ

Financing
Questions

Everything you need to know about payment plans at Maxará Aesthetics.

You can apply right here on this page using the Cherry payment calculator, or ask us during your appointment. The application takes less than 60 seconds and you'll receive an instant decision. All you need is basic personal information — no documents or pay stubs required.

No. Cherry uses a soft credit check to determine eligibility, which does not impact your credit score. A hard inquiry only occurs if you choose to accept and finalize a plan.

Interest rates vary based on the plan you choose and your credit profile. Cherry offers 0% APR promotional plans as well as extended plans with competitive rates. You'll see your exact rate and monthly payment before you commit to anything.

All treatments at Maxará Aesthetics are eligible for Cherry financing — HydraFacials, chemical peels, laser hair removal, microneedling, waxing packages, advanced skincare, and treatment series. There is no minimum purchase amount.

Yes. Cherry allows you to pay off your balance early at any time with no prepayment penalties. You'll only pay interest on the remaining balance, so paying early can save you money.

If your initial application isn't approved, Cherry may offer alternative plan options. You're also welcome to reapply after 30 days. In the meantime, we're happy to discuss other ways to make your treatments work within your budget.
