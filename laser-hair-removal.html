The Technology

The Candela
GentleYAG Pro

Our Nd:YAG laser operates at a 1064nm wavelength, penetrating deep into the hair follicle where melanin absorbs the light energy and converts it to heat — permanently disrupting the structures responsible for hair growth.

What sets the GentleYAG apart is its Dynamic Cooling Device (DCD), which sprays a precise burst of cryogen milliseconds before each laser pulse. This delivers over 50% greater cooling than contact or air methods, ensuring your comfort throughout.

  • 1064nm Nd:YAG wavelength for deep follicle penetration
  • Safe for all skin tones — Fitzpatrick I through VI
  • Dynamic Cooling Device for superior comfort
  • Targets hair in active growth (anagen) phase
  • FDA-cleared for permanent hair reduction

Your Journey

The Path to
Lasting Smoothness

Most clients need 6–12 sessions spaced 4–8 weeks apart. Here's what to expect along the way.

Sessions 1–2

Getting Started

Your initial treatments target visible hair currently in the active growth (anagen) phase. The laser delivers precise energy to each follicle while the cooling device keeps you comfortable. Expect mild redness that typically resolves within a few hours.

What to expect

  • Hair sheds naturally 5–14 days after treatment
  • Some areas may appear unchanged between sessions — that's normal
  • Mild warmth or redness resolves within hours

Sessions 3–4

Building Momentum

As more hair cycles into the growth phase, each session captures follicles that were dormant during earlier treatments. You'll begin to notice visibly thinner regrowth and longer periods of smoothness between appointments.

What to expect

  • Noticeable thinning and slower regrowth
  • Hair comes back finer and lighter
  • Smooth patches appear between sessions

Sessions 5–6

Significant Reduction

By now, a substantial percentage of hair follicles have been permanently disrupted. Many clients find that large portions of the treated area remain smooth between sessions, with only scattered regrowth remaining.

What to expect

  • 50–70% reduction in most areas
  • Extended smoothness between appointments
  • Remaining hair is much finer

Sessions 7–9

Fine-Tuning

These sessions target the most stubborn remaining follicles — those with longer dormant cycles. Your provider may adjust spacing between sessions as the remaining hair becomes more sparse and fine.

What to expect

  • Targeting last remaining stubborn follicles
  • Sessions may be spaced further apart
  • Near-complete smoothness in many areas

Sessions 10–12

Lasting Smoothness

Final treatments maximize your reduction and address any remaining active follicles. After completing your course, most clients transition to occasional maintenance sessions every 6–12 months to keep results flawless.

What to expect

  • Up to 80% permanent hair reduction
  • Transition to maintenance as needed
  • Maintenance sessions every 6–12 months

Before Your Session

Pre-Care
Essentials

Proper preparation ensures the best results and the most comfortable experience. Follow these guidelines before every session.

  • Shave the treatment area 12–24 hours before your appointment
  • Stop waxing, plucking, or threading 4–6 weeks prior
  • Avoid sun exposure and self-tanners for 2–4 weeks
  • Pause retinoids and exfoliating acids 3–5 days before
  • Arrive with clean skin — no lotions, deodorant, or makeup on the area
  • Share any medications or skincare products at booking
aftercare.jpg

After Your Session

Aftercare
Essentials

Your skin will be slightly sensitive after treatment. These simple steps protect your results and keep your skin calm.

  • Mild redness or warmth is normal — resolves within 24 hours
  • Avoid hot showers, saunas, and workouts for 24 hours
  • Apply SPF 30+ to treated areas daily
  • No waxing or plucking between sessions — shaving only
  • Gentle exfoliation after 5–7 days helps natural shedding
  • No treatment over tattoos or irritated/sunburned skin
