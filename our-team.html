Medical Director

Lori Martin,
M.D.

Dr. Lori Martin received her Bachelor of Sciences degree in Biology with a minor in Chemistry from Georgia State University, graduating with honors. She attended Ross University School of Medicine to obtain her Medical Doctorate, then completed her residency at Memorial Health University Medical Center in Savannah, Georgia.

In 2011, Dr. Martin turned her focus to functional medicine, weight loss, aesthetics, and regenerative medicine after recognizing that the traditional medical system often falls short in healing chronic disease and lacks emphasis on preventative care to optimize health, wellness, and longevity. She has completed training at top institutions, including the Institute for Functional Medicine and the American Academy of Anti-Aging Medicine, to provide a more comprehensive approach to patient care.

Her primary focus is regenerative and anti-aging medicine for longevity, which includes aesthetic and therapeutic PRP injections, testosterone replacement, sexual rejuvenation, hair restoration, weight loss through her Longevity Diet Plan, IV nutritional therapy, and non-surgical face and body lifts through personalized plans including cosmetic injection, microneedling, radiofrequency, PDO threads, plasma pen therapy, and medical-grade skincare.

Dr. Martin employs a forward-thinking, individualized approach to practicing medicine and continues to research, educate, and train on the latest regenerative products and techniques available for subtle yet noticeable results. She is passionate about helping patients function optimally and age gracefully utilizing the most natural and least toxic methods possible.
